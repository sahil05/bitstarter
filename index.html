Bring-It-On!

The game has just begun...

Bring-It-On! is an application which allows you to play mini games with your friends. The present plan is to have this app to allow Android users to play with/against each other but there is a plan to make it a cross-platform app supporting iOS, BlackBerry and Windows Phone 8.

Bring-It-On! will use your data plan in the absence of a WiFi connection.
