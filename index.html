Bring-It-On! is an app where you can sync your phone contacts and indulge in fun games with your friends. Providing mindboggling games for the intellectual geeks to the speedy racers, we have them all covered to let you and your friends take a break from the monotonous routine.

Bring-It-On! will be available for a free download for Android phones and will be coming soon to other platforms like iOS and Windows Phone 8. It will utilize your current data plan in the absence of a Wi-Fi connection.

So what are you waiting for? Enhance your gaming skills and put your best foot forward because the game has just begun!
